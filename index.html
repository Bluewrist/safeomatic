About Us

We Are SafeOmatic

We are an independent technical security company and we have established an enviable reputation in providing the highest quality technical security services in Zimbabwe. We are a registered security company with a long working profile in Zimbabwe, specializes in Communication and Security Equipment in the areas of selling, marketing, importing and exporting, distributing, supplying, installing and maintenance of all kinds of Electronic Security and Communication Equipment. Our mandate is to deliver safety and security solutions that are highly cost effective and cost efficient to the fulfillment and satisfaction of our customers. Safeomatic Security Company has the technological knowledge to develop all forms of security system from the Conceptual design to the commissioning and maintenance of the system all in accordance with the client’s requirements. Our systems are adaptable to any location and climatic conditions. We are able to tailor package to,meet individual needs
